About Our Company

Your trusted partner in workplace safety

Committed to Safety Excellence

Isandulelo Safety Supplies is a multi-brand service provider of on-site personal protective equipment solutions in Zululand, launched in 2022. We protect workers by ensuring compliance with regulations, product quality, and comprehensive supporting documentation.

Our goal is to establish fruitful, long-term relationships with business organizations by addressing niche client needs through experience, input, and innovative ideas. We're dedicated to becoming a leading supplier in the workwear, corporate clothing, and safety wear industries across South Africa.

Beyond our extensive product line, we collaborate with clients to develop and conceptualize customized apparel solutions that meet specific requirements. Our corporate workwear and gifting services feature professional branding through embroidery and screen printing.
